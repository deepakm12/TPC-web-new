Why Us?

The Finest Minds

The students at CCET Degree Wing are handpicked from among the brightest minds in the country through Joint Entrance Examination (Main) conducted by CBSE.

The strict selection criteria facilitate that only the best enter the hallowed portal. So, the upcoming aspiring Engineers all can profit from the augmented capacity for learning, understanding, and thinking that can be gained in the four years of Engineering.

These students are the pearls which are found after scaling the ocean of over many applicants. They are then trained and groomed to deal with the newest and most difficult information and concepts which will profit them most and the challenges of the profession.

Experienced Faculty

All the departments have experienced faculty member for each subject to cater to the needs of the students for both theoretical inputs and practical applications.

The faculty members, with years of experience in grooming future engineers, always encourage students to raise their doubts so that students can have a complete understanding of the subject.

Panjab University, Chandigarh

The institution is affiliated to Panjab University, Chandigarh. Panjab University and its affiliated colleges are presently ranked FIRST in India and in top 250 around the world according to the UK based Times Higher Education’s(THE) and Thomas Reuter’s World University Rankings Survey 2013-14.

The curriculum of BE has been designed (Panjab University) with such exhaustive deliberations and discussions with the engineering experts and professionals so as to make the curriculum most closely aligned with the needs and demands of the industry.
